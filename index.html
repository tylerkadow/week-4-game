Crystal Collector
Instructions
You are given a random number to reach when you start the game. By clicking on one of the crystals below, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. If your total score matches the random number, you win! But if you go over the random number, you lose! In every new game, the value of each crystal and the number to reach changes.
